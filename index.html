Protect Our Planet With Global Eco Actions

EcoGuard unites volunteers, enterprises and environmentalists to restore forests, protect wildlife and reduce ecological damage worldwide. Every donation powers real-world conservation work.

Core Conservation Programs

We carry out systematic environmental projects covering forest restoration, animal protection and ocean cleanup across continents.

Forest Reforestation

Plant native trees on degraded land, rebuild woodland ecosystems and absorb carbon dioxide effectively.

Marine Ecosystem Rescue

Clean ocean plastic waste, restore coral reefs and protect endangered marine species from pollution.

Wildlife Sanctuary

Build protected habitats for rare animals, stop illegal hunting and help endangered populations recover.

Waste Recycle Campaign

Promote classified recycling and eco-friendly lifestyles to cut daily pollutant output globally.

Freshwater Protection

Repair polluted rivers and lakes, safeguard clean drinking water sources for local communities.

Global Eco Education

Deliver environmental courses to schools to raise young people’s awareness of nature protection.

What We Have Achieved

With worldwide partners and donors, we keep making measurable progress on ecological restoration every year.

Millions of New Trees

Over 3.2 million saplings planted across tropical and temperate degraded zones.

Hundreds Saved Species

Protected over 280 vulnerable animal species from extinction risks.

120K+ Global Volunteers

Volunteers from 45 countries join our field conservation activities annually.

Reduced Carbon Footprint

Cut nearly 110,000 tons of carbon emission via ecological restoration projects.
